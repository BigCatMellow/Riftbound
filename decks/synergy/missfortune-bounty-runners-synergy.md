## Why This Is A Synergy Deck

Where the Yasuo deck (same lab, Calm/Chaos) plays movement as *tempo*,
this deck plays it as *income* — every move triggers someone:

- `Miss Fortune - Bounty Hunter`: exhaust — any unit gains [Ganking]
  this turn; the Legend is the enabler, so the deck can run payoff
  bodies instead of keyword bodies;
- `Miss Fortune - Captain`: her first move each turn readies something
  else you control — a free untap per turn, forever;
- `Kato the Arm`: moving to a battlefield gives another friendly unit
  his keywords and +Might equal to his — a 3-Might [Deflect] transfer
  at move speed;
- `Nilah - Joyful Ascetic` and `Mister Root`: XP per move, feeding
  `Wily Newtfish` (+1 and Ganking any turn you gained XP) and `Stare
  Down`'s XP rider;
- `Harpoon Squad`: +2 Might whenever it moves *from* a battlefield —
  arriving anywhere as a 6;
- `Fae Porter` and `Stealthy Pursuer`: moves bring friends, so one
  Ganking grant repositions a squad;
- `Miss Fortune - Buccaneer` (chosen): you may deploy units directly to
  open battlefields — the map starts pre-run.

`Bullet Time` converts the convergence into a sweep: pay X runes, deal
X to every enemy unit at one battlefield — sized by however many runes
the turn left standing.

## Legality Notes

- Domain identity: Miss Fortune - Bounty Hunter is Body/Chaos. Every
  main deck card is single-domain Body, single-domain Chaos, or the
  Body/Chaos Signature card `Bullet Time`.
- Copy-limit check: `Miss Fortune - Buccaneer` has 1 chosen-champion
  copy plus 2 main deck copies (3 total, at the limit). `Miss Fortune -
  Captain` is a different card name. No other card exceeds 3 copies.
- Signature check: `Bullet Time` (OGN-268) is Miss Fortune's own
  signature (printed directly after the Legend, OGN-267); 3 copies sit
  exactly at the 3-signature limit and match the Legend's tag.
- Banned/errata check: no card used here is marked Banned in the local
  card database.
- Champion tag: `Miss Fortune - Buccaneer` and `Miss Fortune - Captain`
  are inferred to carry the `Miss Fortune` champion tag from card name
  and the Legend's own name, per this project's convention. The source
  export has no explicit tag column — confirm the printed tag before
  tournament play.

## How To Play

1. Deploy wide early — `Buccaneer`'s open-battlefield deployment means
   turn-2 map presence the opponent has to answer everywhere.
2. Move something every turn, even without an attack in mind: `Nilah`
   and `Mister Root` bank XP, `Captain` untaps a friend, and
   `Harpoon Squad` grows in transit.
3. The Legend's grant goes to payoff bodies, not natural runners —
   `Kato the Arm` with Ganking is a repeatable stat transfer, `Wily
   Newtfish` upgrades itself the same turn.
4. `Fae Porter` plus `Stealthy Pursuer` turn one move into a migration —
   assemble a squad at the weakest battlefield, not the strongest.
5. Hold `Bullet Time` until runes are idle at end of a big turn — X=3
   into a clustered board is a one-Energy massacre, especially after
   `Targon's Peak` refunds the runes.
6. `Stare Down` before committing: clearing smaller units to base means
   the squad arrives to an empty (or overmatched) battlefield.

## Mulligan

Keep:

- one cheap runner (`Mister Root`, `Nilah`, `Loyal Pup`);
- one 4-cost payoff (`Kato`, `Harpoon Squad`, `Wily Newtfish`);
- `Stacked Deck` or a cheap move spell.

Avoid keeping `Miss Fortune - Captain` plus multiple 4-drops — the deck
wants motion by turn 2, not a traffic jam in hand.

## Watch Outs

- The Legend's Ganking lasts **this turn** — grant it when the move is
  ready to happen, not on spec.
- `Kato the Arm`'s transfer copies his *keywords and Might* this turn —
  it doesn't stack twice on the same target from two arrivals (a second
  arrival re-applies, it doesn't add).
- `Miss Fortune - Captain`'s ready is her **first move each turn** only
  — sequence her move before spending other untap effects.
- `Bullet Time` costs runes, not Energy, for its X — it competes with
  Power costs, so count what the turn's plays already spent.
- Moving is not attacking: arriving at a contested battlefield starts a
  combat only per the normal rules — `Stare Down` and `Temptation`
  shape who's home when you arrive.
- XP here has no Level payoffs beyond `Wily Newtfish` — spend `Stare
  Down`'s and `Grim Resolve`'s XP value as icing, don't build turns
  around banking it.

## Upgrade Paths

- `Megatusk` (spend 3 XP: your units here gain Ganking) turns the XP
  bank into mass mobility if the subtheme grows.
- `Sivir - Mercenary` (+2 and Ganking after spending 2+ runes) fits the
  rune-heavy `Bullet Time` turns.
- `Void Assault` is Kha'Zix's signature and illegal here despite the
  perfect fit — the movement-forcing slot belongs to `Temptation` and
  `Call to Battle` in this shell.
- A harder-aggro build adds `Dauntless Vanguard` (play to occupied
  enemy battlefields) and trims the XP package.
