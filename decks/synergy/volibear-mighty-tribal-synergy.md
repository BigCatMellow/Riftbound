## Why This Is A Synergy Deck

`[Mighty]` (5+ Might) is a genuine shared threshold in this card pool, and
this deck stacks four separate payoffs onto crossing it: the Legend channels
a free rune every time a Mighty unit is played, `Jaull-Fish` gets cheaper
for every Mighty unit already on board, `Kadregrin the Infernal` and
`Show of Strength` both draw a card per Mighty unit, and `Sunken Temple`
rewards conquering with one. Cheap Might-boost spells (`Punch First`,
`Primal Strength`) exist purely to push a smaller unit over the threshold
on demand, not as combat tricks in their own right.

## How To Play

1. Play naturally-Mighty cheap bodies (`Kraken Hunter`) early to start
   triggering the Legend's free rune channel immediately.
2. Use `Punch First` or `Primal Strength` to push a smaller unit over the
   Mighty line right before a combat or a conquer at `Sunken Temple`.
3. Land `Jaull-Fish` once a couple of Mighty units are already out — its
   discount stacks fast.
4. Time `Kadregrin the Infernal` and `Show of Strength` for a turn when
   your board already has several Mighty units, not as an opener.
5. Use `Challenge` to trade a big Mighty unit's stats directly into a
   removal effect against a smaller enemy unit.
6. Close with `Volibear - Furious` once the board is stable — its AOE
   attack trigger can clear a crowded battlefield in one swing.

## Mulligan

Keep:

- one naturally Mighty cheap body: `Kraken Hunter`;
- one Might-push spell: `Punch First`;
- one cheap curve filler: `Pit Rookie`, `Chemtech Enforcer`, or
  `Sea Monkey`.

Avoid keeping `Kadregrin the Infernal`, `Jaull-Fish`, or
`Volibear - Furious` without any early Mighty pieces already in hand —
all three want the board already Mighty to be worth their slot.

## Watch Outs

- `[Mighty]` is checked in the moment, not tracked as a permanent status —
  a unit buffed to 5+ Might "this turn" by `Punch First` or
  `Primal Strength` stops being Mighty once that temporary bonus expires
  at end of turn, taking `Jaull-Fish`'s discount and similar payoffs with
  it.
- The Legend's channel ability is optional and player-triggered each time
  a Mighty unit is played — remember to actually activate it, since it
  doesn't happen automatically.
- Several of this deck's best payoffs (`Kadregrin`, `Jaull-Fish`,
  `Volibear - Furious` itself) are genuinely expensive — this is a
  midrange-to-late-game plan, not a fast aggro deck.
- `Challenge` and similar Might-trade spells hurt your own unit too — only
  use them when you're confident your unit's Might is higher than the
  target's.

## Upgrade Paths

- Add `Fiora - Grand Duelist`-style Mighty payoffs from other domains if
  splashing is acceptable, since Mighty-matters support exists outside
  Fury/Body too.
- Add more permanent buff sources if temporary Might pushes feel too
  fragile against removal timed for end of turn.
- For a lower-complexity version, cut the top-end trio
  (`Kadregrin the Infernal`, `Jaull-Fish`, `Deadbloom Predator`) and build
  a more straightforward midrange Mighty-stats deck without chasing the
  most expensive payoffs.
