## Why This Is A Synergy Deck

`Pyke - Bloodharbor Ripper` reads like a drawback (pay 1, return your own
unit to hand) until every unit's best text is its on-play line:

- bounce `Mischievous Marai` and replay it — 2 more damage;
- bounce `Cemetery Attendant` and replay it — another unit back from
  the trash;
- bounce `Bewitching Spirit` — another forced discard;
- and each activation mints a Gold token, which is exactly the loose
  rune you need to replay what you just picked up.

`Pyke - Returned` mints more Gold whenever enemies die at his
battlefield, `Treasure Hunter` mints it for moving, and `Ripper's Bay`
channels a rune every time a unit bounces. The deck's mana engine *is*
its play pattern.

## How To Play

1. Open on cheap Hidden/ETB units and start trading: `Mischievous
   Marai`, `Windsinger`, `Treasure Hunter`.
2. From turn 2-3 on, activate the Legend nearly every turn. Default
   target: whichever unit's on-play effect is best *right now* — usually
   `Marai` (damage) or `Cemetery Attendant` (regrowth).
3. Spend Gold on Power costs when replaying — the tokens exist so that
   bouncing never costs you the turn's development.
4. Park `Pyke - Returned` at the battlefield where fights happen; with
   `Hextech Ray`, `Marai` pings, and `Death from Below`, enemy units die
   at his feet on your schedule.
5. `Death from Below` on anything with 3 or less Might is almost always
   right — it comes back. Save the non-recurring cast for a real bomb.
6. `Angler Beast` after a wide trade resets their board and your ETB
   hands — sequence your own replays *after* it resolves.

## Mulligan

Keep:

- two cheap ETB units (`Marai`, `Windsinger`, `Treasure Hunter`);
- one piece of interaction (`Gust`, `Hextech Ray`);
- `Cemetery Attendant` if you already have units to trade off.

Avoid keeping double 5-drops — the deck's early game is cheap bodies
cycling through the door.

## Watch Outs

- The Legend only reaches units **at battlefields** — a unit sitting in
  base can't be bounced for value, so end turns with your loop piece
  deployed.
- Bouncing your only unit at a battlefield before scoring surrenders the
  hold — count points before pulling the door shut.
- `Death from Below`'s re-buy checks the victim's Might: killing a
  pumped 4-Might unit means no recursion, even if it's printed at 3.
- `Pyke - Returned`'s Gold trigger is once per turn and location-bound —
  spreading him away from the action wastes it.
- Gold tokens add **Any Rune**, not Energy — they cover Power costs and
  rune-priced re-buys (`Death from Below`, `Flame Chompers`-style
  costs), not your Energy curve.

## Upgrade Paths

- `Flame Chompers` (replays itself from discard for a Fury Rune) adds a
  discard-side loop if the deck leans harder on `Lunar Boon` and `Zaun
  Warrens`.
- `Perched Grimwyrm` (4-cost 5-Might, playable only to a battlefield you
  conquered this turn) rewards a more aggressive build.
- `Black Market Broker` mints Gold off facedown plays if you add more
  [Hidden] cards.
- A control build could swap `Bewitching Spirit` up to 3 copies plus
  `Mindsplitter` for a dedicated hand-attack plan the Legend re-buys.
