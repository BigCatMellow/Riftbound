## Why This Is A Synergy Deck

Every other equipment deck pays for each attach; this one recycles:

- `Jax - Grandmaster at Arms` (Legend): exhaust — move an **attached**
  Equipment to another unit free; or 1 Energy + exhaust — attach a
  **detached** one. The armory never sits idle;
- `Jax - Unrelenting` (chosen): every attach to him → pay 1, draw 1 —
  the Legend's juggle becomes a card engine by simply routing swords
  through Jax on the way to their real bearer... and then moving them
  on;
- `Aphelios - Exalted`: every attach to him → ready 2 runes, channel 1,
  or buff — the juggle mints mana instead of cards on his stops;
- `Jax - Unmatched`: all your Equipment gain [Quick-Draw] — swords
  played at reaction speed mid-combat, then juggled next turn;
- `Akshan - Mischievous`: pay two Body Runes on play → steal an enemy
  gear and wear it while he lives — their sword joins your rotation;
- the [Weaponmaster] crew (`Veteran Poro`, `Lucian - Merciless`,
  `Combat Chef`, `Yone - Blademaster`) each arrive with a discounted
  attach — more juggle stops;
- `Counter Strike` (signature) protects whichever bearer currently
  holds the crown jewels.

## Legality Notes

- Domain identity: Jax - Grandmaster at Arms is Calm/Body. Every main
  deck card is single-domain Calm, single-domain Body, or the Calm/Body
  Signature card `Counter Strike`.
- Copy-limit check: `Jax - Unrelenting` has 1 chosen-champion copy plus
  2 main deck copies (3 total, at the limit). `Jax - Unmatched` is a
  different card name. No other card exceeds 3 copies.
- Signature check: `Counter Strike` (SFD-194) is Jax's own signature
  (printed directly after the Legend, SFD-193); 3 copies sit exactly at
  the 3-signature limit and match the Legend's tag.
- Banned/errata check: no card used here is marked Banned in the local
  card database.
- Champion tag: `Jax - Unrelenting` and `Jax - Unmatched` are inferred
  to carry the `Jax` champion tag from card name and the Legend's own
  name, per this project's convention. The source export has no
  explicit tag column — confirm the printed tag before tournament play.

## How To Play

1. Open with cheap gear and a bearer (`Veteran Poro` + `Doran's
   Shield`), then add `Jax - Unrelenting` and start routing: every
   sword's journey passes through Jax (pay 1, draw 1) before settling
   on its bearer.
2. The Legend's free move is the tempo engine: attack with the armed
   unit, then juggle the sword onto an untapped defender before the
   opponent's turn — the same +3 fights twice a round.
3. `Aphelios` stops convert attaches to mana: route a sword through him
   on turns you need the runes back.
4. `Jax - Unmatched` changes combat: a `Trinity Force` at Quick-Draw
   speed mid-showdown is +2 the opponent never priced in.
5. `Akshan` with two spare Body Runes robs their Ornn/Azir/Lucian-style
   decks outright — the stolen sword juggles like your own while he
   lives.
6. `Counter Strike` guards the alpha bearer; `Punch First` plus the
   juggled stats closes.

## Mulligan

Keep:

- one cheap bearer (`Veteran Poro`, `Lucian - Merciless`);
- one or two cheap gear (`Doran's Shield`, `Warmog's Armor`,
  `Hexdrinker`);
- `Jax - Unrelenting` if the curve allows.

Avoid keeping gear-only or bearer-only hands — the deck is a marriage,
not a monologue.

## Watch Outs

- The Legend's two abilities share his exhaust — **one juggle per
  turn**: either re-attach a detached sword (1 Energy) or move an
  attached one (free), not both.
- `Jax - Unrelenting`'s draw costs 1 Energy per attach and only
  triggers on attaches **to him** — routing everything through Jax is
  a habit, not a rule; skip the stop when the Energy matters more.
- `Svellsongur` copying `Jax - Unrelenting`'s text puts an
  attach-trigger **on the Equipment** — whether an Equipment "you
  attach an Equipment to" can meaningfully hold that text is not
  settled by the local rules extract; treat the copy line as a bonus
  curio, not a combo piece, per this project's explicit-ambiguity
  practice.
- `Akshan`'s theft lasts **while he's on the board** — the stolen gear
  goes home when he dies, even if you juggled it elsewhere; don't
  build a turn on borrowed swords without protection.
- [Weaponmaster] discounts trigger on the bearer being **played** —
  the discount can't be reused by the Legend's later juggling.
- Equipment Might is the bearer's while attached — juggling away mid
  combat after damage assignment doesn't undo a fight; move swords
  between fights, not during them (unless Quick-Draw plays a *new*
  one).

## Upgrade Paths

- `Highlander` and `Alpha Strike` are Master Yi's signatures and
  illegal despite the domain match — protection stays `Counter Strike`
  plus `Zhonya's Hourglass` if needed.
- `Mistfall` (buff → ready) pairs with `Aphelios`' buff mode for an
  untap subtheme.
- `Gearhead` is Mind-domain and belongs to the Ornn shell — the
  doubling effect cannot port here; this deck's scaling is breadth of
  bearers, not depth of bonuses.
- `Master Yi - Meditative` (+4 while 8+ runes) rewards the Aphelios
  mana engine in a slower build.
