## Why This Is A Synergy Deck

`[Weaponmaster]` (attach an Equipment for a discount the moment a unit is
played) and the Legend's blanket Assault-from-Equipment both key off the same
resource: how much Equipment you have on the board and who it's attached to.
`Angle Shot` and `Strike Down` let you move Equipment between units or cash
one in for direct damage, so gear isn't stuck on whichever body drew it —
it circulates to wherever the game needs it. This is a genuinely different
mechanical identity from this project's other decks: none of them lean on
Equip costs or the Weaponmaster keyword at all.

## How To Play

1. Play cheap Weaponmaster bodies (`Veteran Poro`, `Sentinel Adept`) alongside
   cheap Equipment (`Doran's Blade`, `Hexdrinker`) to get an equipped
   attacker online early.
2. Land `Lucian - Merciless` once you have Equipment to attach — every
   attached piece grants Assault from the Legend, and its first conquer each
   turn readies it for another swing.
3. Use `Angle Shot` or `Strike Down` to move Equipment off a unit that's
   about to trade unfavorably and onto your next attacker instead of losing
   the gear with the body.
4. Play `Jax - Unrelenting` when you have Equipment ready to attach — the
   card draw pays for itself immediately.
5. Save `Yone - Blademaster` for an uncontrolled battlefield conquer; the
   reach damage scales with however much Equipment-boosted Might it's
   carrying.
6. Use `Long Sword`'s Quick-Draw to get a body equipped even without a
   Weaponmaster unit in play yet.

## Mulligan

Keep:

- one cheap Weaponmaster body: `Veteran Poro` or `Sentinel Adept`;
- one cheap piece of Equipment: `Doran's Blade`, `Hexdrinker`, or
  `Long Sword`;
- `Lucian - Merciless` only if you also have Equipment or a body to pair
  with it.

Avoid keeping `Armed Assailant` or `Combat Chef` without early Equipment
already available to attach.

## Watch Outs

- The Legend's Assault bonus only applies while the equipped unit is
  attacking — it does nothing for a piece of gear sitting on a defender.
- `Strike Down` detaches the Equipment after dealing damage — plan to
  re-equip it elsewhere with `Angle Shot` or a fresh Weaponmaster body,
  don't expect it to stay put.
- Losing an equipped unit in combat loses the Might bonus with it unless the
  Equipment is moved first; consider detaching valuable gear before a fight
  you expect to lose.
- `Relentless Pursuit`'s "move to base on conquer" clause only lasts the
  turn it's granted — it doesn't stick permanently to the unit.
- Nothing in this list has `[Ganking]`. Standard Move only goes Base↔
  Battlefield, never Battlefield-to-Battlefield, without it — once your
  board commits to one battlefield, contesting a second one costs a full
  extra turn (recall to base, then move again) rather than a single hop.
  Plan which battlefield to commit to accordingly. Confirmed in
  `simulations/SIM-0004-teemo-vs-lucian-rules-smoke.md`.

## Upgrade Paths

- Add `Akshan - Mischievous` (Body) to steal an opponent's Equipment outright
  instead of only recirculating your own.
- Add more card draw if the deck runs out of gas after its early Equipment
  is spent.
- For a more advanced version, build around `Rell - Magnetic`'s free-equip
  attack trigger as the deck's main plan instead of a supporting piece,
  which asks for tighter Energy sequencing than this list needs.
