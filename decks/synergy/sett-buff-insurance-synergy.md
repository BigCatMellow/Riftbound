## Why This Is A Synergy Deck

Buffs in this pool are usually a small, one-time +1 Might with no further
upside once applied — this deck is built around treating a buff as a
banked resource instead. `Sett - The Boss` spends a dying unit's buff to
save it from death entirely (heal, exhaust, recall) rather than just
losing the unit; `Kraken Hunter`, `Wildclaw Shaman`, `Call to Glory`, and
`Wallop` all let a buff be spent as an alternate cost for something else
useful. `Sett - Kingpin` and `Lee Sin - Centered` both reward simply having
several buffed units around at once, which this deck's cheap curve of
buff-granting bodies is built to produce quickly.

## How To Play

1. Spread cheap buffs early (`Pit Rookie`, `Cithria of Cloudfield`,
   `Trifarian Gloryseeker`) to build a buffed board before bigger payoffs
   land.
2. Get `Sett - Kingpin` down once a few buffed units are already out — its
   Might scales immediately with what's already there.
3. Use `Kraken Hunter`, `Wildclaw Shaman`, `Call to Glory`, or `Wallop` to
   spend a buff for real value (a discount, readying, a free spell) instead
   of just holding it passively.
4. Let the Legend save a buffed unit that's about to die in combat — it's
   an automatic option, not a card, so always check if it's available
   before accepting a trade as final.
5. Group buffed units at one battlefield to get full value from
   `Lee Sin - Centered`'s local anthem and `Sett - Kingpin`'s own scaling.

## Mulligan

Keep:

- one cheap buff-granter: `Pit Rookie`, `Cithria of Cloudfield`, or
  `Trifarian Gloryseeker`;
- one buff-spender: `Kraken Hunter`, `Wallop`, or `Call to Glory`;
- `Sett - Kingpin` only with an early buff-granter already in hand.

Avoid keeping `Peak Guardian`, `Lee Sin - Centered`, or `Udyr - Wildman`
without any early buff sources — all three want a buffed board already
forming.

## Watch Outs

- A unit can only have "a buff" once ("if it doesn't already have one") —
  stacking multiple buff sources on the same unit past the first does
  nothing extra, confirmed as a hard per-unit cap in
  `simulations/SIM-0005-leona-vs-renata-rules-smoke.md`. Spread buffs
  across different units rather than piling them on one.
- The Legend's save-a-buffed-unit ability costs Any Rune and consumes the
  unit's buff — it isn't free, and it only works while the Legend itself
  can be exhausted (it needs to be ready).
- Spending a buff as an alternate cost (`Call to Glory`, `Wallop`,
  `Kraken Hunter`) removes that buff from the unit — it can no longer be
  saved by the Legend's ability afterward.
- `Sett - Kingpin`'s bonus only counts buffed units at *its own*
  battlefield, not your whole board — keep buffed units together with it
  rather than spread across multiple locations.

## Upgrade Paths

- Add `Karma - Channeler` (Order) for a recycle-into-buff engine if the
  deck wants more late-game card selection alongside its buff plan.
- Add more Reaction-speed buff-spenders if the meta rewards holding up
  interaction during the opponent's turn.
- For a more advanced version, build explicitly around banking multiple
  buffs before spending any of them, timing several buff-spenders in a
  single turn — which asks for tighter sequencing than this list needs.
