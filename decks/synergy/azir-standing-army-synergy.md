## Why This Is A Synergy Deck

Token decks usually go wide and stay small; equipment decks usually go
tall on one body that removal then answers. `Azir - Emperor of the
Sands` fuses the plans so each fixes the other's weakness:

- every Equipment you play triggers the Legend: 1 Energy for a 2-Might
  Sand Soldier — the gear brings its own bearer;
- every Sand Soldier has [Weaponmaster], so the token can immediately
  pick up an Equipment at a rune discount — the bearer brings its own
  sword. A `Sterak's Gage` on a "token" makes it a 5-Might threat;
- `Blade of the Ruined King`'s equip cost (kill a friendly unit) eats a
  spare soldier — the army is also ammunition;
- `Renata Glasc - Industrialist` makes your tokens enter ready, so
  `Guards!` from facedown is a ready blocker mid-showdown;
- `Arise!` plays a soldier **per Equipment you control** and readies
  two — lategame it's a 4-6 body swing that all wear gear next turn;
- `Azir - Sovereign` then attacks and pulls the whole token army to his
  battlefield at once.

Removal that kills one big equipped unit answers nothing: the swords
outlive their bearers, and the Legend keeps printing bearers.

## How To Play

1. Every turn from turn 2 on, try to play one Equipment before ending
   your Main Phase, then spend 1 Energy on the Legend — one soldier per
   turn is the deck's metronome.
2. Use each soldier's [Weaponmaster] on arrival: the discounted equip
   means the token leaves the assembly line already armed.
3. `Renata Glasc - Industrialist` changes what your spells mean —
   `Guards!` from facedown becomes an armed, ready blocker in the middle
   of a showdown.
4. Feed `Blade of the Ruined King` a spare base soldier, not a deployed
   one; the Legend replaces base bodies for 1 Energy.
5. Count your Equipment before casting `Arise!` — at three or more gear
   it's usually the best six Energy the deck can spend, and the two
   readies make it playable on defense.
6. The kill turn: `Azir - Sovereign` attacks (Accelerated if needed) and
   pulls every token to his battlefield; `Rally the Troops` the same
   turn makes the pile bigger.

## Mulligan

Keep:

- one cheap gear (`Doran's Shield`, `Eye of the Herald`) — the Legend
  needs it to start the metronome;
- one soldier source (`Desert's Call`, `Guards!`, `Royal Guard`);
- one engine champion (`Renata`, `Aphelios`) if the curve allows.

Avoid keeping `Arise!` or `B.F. Sword` in openers with no cheap gear —
the engine starts at one Energy, not four.

## Watch Outs

- The Legend's soldier ability needs an Equipment played **this turn**
  and puts the token **in your base**, not at a battlefield — plan a
  move or an `Azir - Sovereign` attack to get them into play.
- [Weaponmaster] triggers when the unit is **played** — a soldier that
  arrived last turn can't retroactively use the discount; equip on
  arrival or pay full price later.
- `Blade of the Ruined King`'s kill cost is a real cost — with no spare
  body it can't be equipped at all.
- `Svellsongur` copies text only **while attached** — moving it resets
  what it does, and copying a vanilla body copies nothing.
- Tokens vanish if they leave the board — every bounce or recall effect
  deletes a soldier outright. Keep the real units for those risks.
- `Arise!` counts Equipment you **control**, attached or not — but
  killed swords (fed to Blade, killed by `Detonate`-style effects) count
  for nothing. Cast it while the armory is intact.

## Upgrade Paths

- `Aphelios` + `Svellsongur` + `Jax - Unmatched` is already a
  mini-combo shell; a heavier build adds `Forge of the Fluft` (legends
  gain an attach ability) for a fourth attach engine.
- `Corina Veraza` and `Recruit the Vanguard` splash the Recruit-token
  package if you want raw width over armed width.
- `Nami - Headstrong` (hold → next unit enters ready and buffed) suits
  a more defensive build.
- Against gear removal metas, add `Aspiring Engineer`-style recursion
  from the Mind pool is unavailable here — instead favor `Veiled Temple`
  (battlefield: conquer → ready a friendly gear, may detach) to rebuild.
