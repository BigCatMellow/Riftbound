## Why This Is A Synergy Deck

The tribal payoffs read tag *diversity*, not just count:

- `Ivern - Green Father`: conquer or hold → exhaust → replace that
  battlefield with a **Brush** token, where Bird, Cat, Dog, Poro, and
  Ivern units have +1 Might — terraforming as a tribal anthem;
- `Daisy!` (signature unit): 9 Energy, minus 1 per tag among Bird, Cat,
  Dog, and Poro you control — with three tags out she's a 6-cost that
  **enters ready**;
- `Friendship`: +1 Might per animal tag among your units — the going
  rate is +2 or +3 for one Energy;
- `Ivern - Friend to All` gains a chosen animal tag on play and scores
  a bonus point on conquer/hold if all four tags are among your units;
- `Poro Herder` (buff + draw off any Poro), `Ultrasoft Poro`
  (repeatable Deflect-Bird tokens), `Starhound` (regrows any animal),
  and `Undying Loyalty` (discounted animal reanimation) keep the zoo
  stocked.

## Legality Notes

- Domain identity: Ivern - Green Father is Calm/Order. Every main deck
  card is single-domain Calm, single-domain Order, or the Calm/Order
  Signature card `Daisy!`.
- Copy-limit check: `Ivern - Nurturer` has 1 chosen-champion copy plus
  2 main deck copies (3 total, at the limit). `Ivern - Friend to All`
  is a different card name. No other card exceeds 3 copies.
- Signature check: `Daisy!` (UNL-196) is Ivern's own signature (printed
  directly after the Legend, UNL-195); 3 copies sit exactly at the
  3-signature limit and match the Legend's tag.
- Banned/errata check: no card used here is marked Banned in the local
  card database.
- **Tag data gap (important)**: the local card export has NO tag
  column. Poros are identified by name; Birds by explicit token text.
  **No card in Calm/Order is verifiable as Cat or Dog** — `Trusty
  Ramhound` is *presumed* Dog from its name, and the fourth tag must
  come from `Ivern - Friend to All`'s chosen tag. Every effect below
  that counts tags (Daisy!'s discount, Friendship, the Friend-to-All
  scoring line) therefore carries uncertainty beyond Bird+Poro. The
  deck is built to function on Bird+Poro alone; treat the 3rd/4th tags
  as upside pending confirmation against physical cards.
- Champion tag: `Ivern - Nurturer` and `Ivern - Friend to All` are
  inferred to carry the `Ivern` champion tag from card name and the
  Legend's own name, per this project's convention.

## How To Play

1. Swarm early — Poros are cheap, and `Poro Herder` keeps the cards
   flowing while the board widens.
2. Exhaust the Legend on your most defensible conquer/hold: Brush's +1
   applies to (almost) the whole deck, and it persists until scored.
3. Keep the tags diverse on purpose: a `Frisky Hunter` (Bird+body) or
   `Trusty Ramhound` played before `Friendship` or `Daisy!` changes the
   math by a full point.
4. `Ultrasoft Poro` at a Brush battlefield prints 2-Might Deflect Birds
   — the swarm quality rises with the terrain.
5. `Daisy!` is the payoff: at three tags she's 6 Energy, enters ready,
   and swings immediately. Count tags before the turn, not during.
6. `Ivern - Friend to All` claims whichever tag the board is missing;
   his bonus point on each conquer/hold is the win condition in long
   games — if the tag data holds up (see Legality Notes).

## Mulligan

Keep:

- two cheap animals (`Stalwart Poro`, `Daring Poro`, `Carrion
  Dredger`, `Trusty Ramhound`);
- `Poro Herder` (the engine 3-drop);
- `Poro Snax` or `Friendship` as the cheap spell.

Avoid keeping `Daisy!` or double 5-drops — the zoo populates bottom-up.

## Watch Outs

- **The tag data gap is real** (see Legality Notes): before playing
  this deck seriously, verify on physical cards which units are
  actually tagged Cat and Dog. Everything except the 4-tag lines works
  on Bird+Poro alone.
- The Legend **exhausts** to terraform — one Brush per turn, and the
  Brush token "can be swapped back when scored," so the anthem is
  strongest on battlefields you hold rather than score immediately.
- `Daisy!`'s discount counts tags **you control** at cast time — tokens
  count, but a swept board means full price.
- `Friendship` counts tags *among your units*, once each — four Poros
  is +1, not +4; diversity beats duplication.
- `Ivern - Friend to All`'s chosen tag lives on him — if he dies, the
  fourth tag usually dies with him; `Starhound` and `Undying Loyalty`
  can bring him back.
- Bird tokens evaporate if bounced — they're presence, not value.

## Upgrade Paths

- `Zenith Blade` and `Arise!` are Leona's and Azir's signatures —
  illegal here despite the domain match.
- If Cat-tagged Calm/Order cards are confirmed to exist in the pool,
  slot the cheapest one over `Bonds of Strength` immediately — a
  verified fourth tag upgrades three cards at once.
- `Enthusiastic Promoter` (hold → buff all units here) deepens the
  hold plan.
- `Soraka - Wanderer` protects the herd's key bodies in a defensive
  build.
