## Why This Is A Synergy Deck

Each engine piece multiplies the others instead of just adding:

- `Viktor - Herald of the Arcane`: 1 Energy, exhaust — a Recruit token,
  every single turn, no conditions;
- `Zilean - Time Mage`: once per turn a token play becomes **two**
  tokens — it doubles the Legend, `Faithful Manufactor`, `Noxian
  Drummer`, and one token of a `Recruit the Vanguard` batch;
- `Viktor - Innovator`: play a card on an opponent's turn, get a Recruit
  — and this deck runs nine cheap Reactions (`Siphon Power`, `Downstage
  Dramatics`, `Bonds of Strength`, `Stupefy`) precisely to keep printing
  during their turns;
- `Viktor - Leader` (chosen): every *non*-Recruit friendly death makes
  another Recruit — the army regrows from its own casualties;
- `Soul Shepherd` (+1 Might to tokens) and `Renata Glasc -
  Industrialist` (tokens enter ready) convert width into rate: 2-Might
  bodies that can block the turn they arrive.

`Siphon Power` is the battle plan in one card: +1 to your whole
battlefield, -1 to theirs, at reaction speed — with ten tokens in play
that's a ±10 swing for 2 Energy.

## Legality Notes

- Domain identity: Viktor - Herald of the Arcane is Mind/Order. Every
  main deck card is single-domain Mind, single-domain Order, or the
  Mind/Order Signature card `Siphon Power`.
- Copy-limit check: `Viktor - Leader` has 1 chosen-champion copy plus 2
  main deck copies (3 total, at the limit). `Viktor - Innovator` is a
  different card name (3 copies legal). No other card exceeds 3 copies.
- Signature check: `Siphon Power` (OGN-266) is Viktor's own signature
  (printed directly after the Legend, OGN-265); 3 copies sit exactly at
  the 3-signature limit and match the Legend's tag.
- Banned/errata check: no card used here is marked Banned in the local
  card database.
- Champion tag: `Viktor - Leader` and `Viktor - Innovator` are inferred
  to carry the `Viktor` champion tag from card name and the Legend's own
  name, per this project's convention. The source export has no explicit
  tag column — confirm the printed tag before tournament play.

## How To Play

1. Activate the Legend every turn from turn 1 — a body per turn adds up
   faster than it looks, and holding battlefields is this deck's economy.
2. Position `Zilean - Time Mage` where your tokens are being played;
   his double applies to tokens played *at his battlefield*, so keep the
   factory centralized.
3. With `Viktor - Innovator` down, hold your reactions for the
   opponent's turn on purpose — `Downstage Dramatics` and `Stupefy` are
   costed to print Recruits while you sleep.
4. Sequence anthem before flood when you can: `Soul Shepherd` then
   `Recruit the Vanguard` is four 2-Might bodies.
5. `Siphon Power` wins showdowns you have no business winning — cast it
   after blockers are declared at your widest battlefield.
6. Win by hold-scoring wide (`Altar to Unity` compounds) or by
   assembling seven units at `The Grand Plaza`.

## Mulligan

Keep:

- one 3-cost token maker (`Faithful Manufactor`, `Vanguard Captain`,
  `Noxian Drummer`);
- one engine champion (`Zilean`, `Renata`, `Innovator`);
- `Stupefy` or `Downstage Dramatics` for early interaction.

Avoid keeping `Corina Veraza` or `Vanguard Armory` in openers without
early plays — the machine needs its first gears by turn 3.

## Watch Outs

- `Zilean`'s double is once per turn and location-bound — a `Recruit the
  Vanguard` split across battlefields only doubles the token played at
  *his* battlefield, and only one of them.
- `Viktor - Leader` triggers on **non-Recruit** deaths only — the token
  army regrowing itself would be a loop, and it isn't one.
- Recruits are tokens: bounced or recalled, they evaporate. Against
  bounce-heavy decks (see `pyke-revolving-door-synergy.md`), keep real
  units at the scoring battlefield.
- `Siphon Power`'s -1 respects the minimum of 1 — it shrinks, it doesn't
  sweep; pair it with combat, not instead of combat.
- `Vanguard Armory` is 7 Energy up front for a slow drip — it's the
  late-game engine, not a curve play.

## Upgrade Paths

- `Karthus - Eternal` doubles `Machine Evangel`'s Deathknell (six
  Recruits) if the deck drifts toward the LeBlanc shell's death themes.
- `Lillia - Protector of Dreams` (tokens have [Tank]) makes the wall
  literal against aggro.
- `Ultrasoft Poro` and the Bird-token package add token types that all
  ride the same anthems.
- `Hostile Takeover` (Mind/Order, 5) is *Renata's* signature, not
  Viktor's — it cannot go in this deck; steal effects here must come
  from non-signature cards.
