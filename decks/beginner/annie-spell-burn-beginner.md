## Why This Is Beginner Friendly

This deck introduces spell-based interaction with mostly direct text: deal
damage, draw cards, bounce a unit, or play a unit that enters ready. The pilot
gets to learn how showdowns and removal matter without needing a large hidden
package or a multi-card combo engine.

## Legality Notes

- Domain identity: Annie is Fury/Chaos, and every main deck card is Fury, Chaos,
  or Fury/Chaos.
- Copy-limit check: no main deck card exceeds 3 copies. `Annie - Fiery` has 1
  chosen champion copy plus 2 main deck copies, staying at the 3-copy limit.
- Signature check: the only signature card is `Tibbers`, included as 3 copies
  total.
- Banned/errata check: no card used here is marked banned in the local card
  database. Annie's starter legend appears in `Banned Errata` because it has
  errata text, not because it is banned.

## How To Play

1. Use cheap units to contest early battlefields.
2. Spend damage spells to remove blockers before combat math gets messy.
3. Play `Annie - Fiery` when you can immediately benefit from bonus damage or
   when the opponent is low on answers.
4. Use `Annie - Stubborn` to return the best spell in your trash, usually
   `Void Seeker`, `Firestorm`, or the removal spell that lines up with the board.
5. End turns with Annie's legend trigger in mind: spending runes is less scary
   when you can ready up to 2 of them afterward.
6. Use `Tibbers` when the board is getting out of hand or when a 7 Might unit
   plus mass damage will swing the game.

## Mulligan

Keep:

- one cheap unit: `Blast Corps Cadet`, `Chemtech Enforcer`, `Legion Rearguard`,
  or `Mystic Poro`;
- one cheap spell: `Hextech Ray`, `Incinerate`, or `Get Excited!`;
- one engine or payoff: `Annie - Fiery`, `Annie - Stubborn`, `Void Seeker`, or
  `Crescent Guardian`.

Avoid keeping multiple 6+ cost cards. `Firestorm` and `Tibbers` are powerful,
but the deck needs early plays first.

## Watch Outs

- `Annie - Fiery` increases your spell and ability damage, so sequence her
  before damage spells when possible.
- `Get Excited!` depends on the discarded card's Energy cost. Discarding a
  low-cost card may not kill the target you care about.
- `Tibbers` deals damage to all units at battlefields, including yours. Set up
  for it or use it when you are behind.
- Do not spend all damage spells on small targets if the opponent can play one
  large unit that demands an answer.

## Upgrade Paths

- Add stronger Fury removal if the local meta has larger units.
- Add more Chaos card selection if hands feel inconsistent.
- For a more advanced build, move toward Jinx discard synergies or a hidden-card
  Chaos package. Those have higher ceilings but more sequencing burden.
