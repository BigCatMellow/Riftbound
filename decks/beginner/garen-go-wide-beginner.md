## Why This Is Beginner Friendly

This deck teaches the main board loop without asking the pilot to manage many
hidden cards, complicated reactions, or alternate resource engines. Most cards
either add bodies, make those bodies larger, or provide simple combat tricks.
The goal is easy to see on the table: put multiple units at a battlefield, win
combat, and refill with Garen.

## Legality Notes

- Domain identity: Garen is Body/Order, and every main deck card is Body,
  Order, or Body/Order.
- Copy-limit check: no main deck card exceeds 3 copies. `Garen - Commander` has
  1 chosen champion copy plus 2 main deck copies, staying at the 3-copy limit.
- Signature check: the only signature card is `Decisive Strike`, included as 3
  copies total.
- Banned/errata check: no card used here is marked banned in the local card
  database. Re-check `Banned Errata` before playing in a formal event.

## How To Play

1. Build a base with cheap units and token makers.
2. Move multiple units to the same battlefield instead of spreading too thin.
3. Try to conquer with at least four units so Garen draws 2.
4. Use `Garen - Commander`, `Kinkou Monk`, `Doran's Blade`, and `Decisive Strike`
   to make small units win combat.
5. After drawing from Garen, keep repeating the same loop: add bodies, group
   them, conquer, refill.

## Mulligan

Keep:

- at least one 2-cost card: `Trusty Ramhound`, `Pit Rookie`, `Forge of the
  Future`, or `Doran's Blade`;
- at least one body maker: `Faithful Manufactor`, `Vanguard Captain`, `Royal
  Guard`, or `Recruit the Vanguard`;
- one payoff or stabilizer: `Kinkou Monk`, `Garen - Commander`, `Decisive
  Strike`, or `Back to Back`.

Avoid keeping too many 6-cost cards unless the rest of the hand already plays
early units.

## Watch Outs

- Do not split units across too many battlefields early. Garen only draws when
  you conquer with 4 or more units at that battlefield.
- `Vanguard Captain` needs another card played first to get its Legion bonus.
  When possible, play a cheap card before it.
- `Decisive Strike` is best when you already have several units. Do not spend it
  for a small bonus unless it wins an important combat.
- Board wipes and mass damage punish overcommitting. If the opponent represents
  that kind of effect, hold one token maker back.

## Upgrade Paths

- Add stronger Body/Order rares if you want more power, especially units that
  enter ready or produce extra bodies.
- Add more equipment if the deck needs larger single attackers, but keep enough
  unit makers to trigger Garen.
- For a more advanced version, add reaction-heavy protection and battlefield
  movement cards. That improves ceiling but makes the deck less beginner
  friendly.
