## Why This Is Beginner Friendly

Stun and Buff are both single-sentence keywords with no hidden information or
multi-step chains: "doesn't deal combat damage this turn" and "+1 Might if it
doesn't have one." The Legend directly rewards the deck's removal (stun) with
its payoff (buff), so a new player always knows what their stun spells are
for beyond just answering a threat. Most cards are straightforward units and
Action-speed spells, with only one or two Reaction/Hidden cards to learn
later.

## How To Play

1. Use cheap stun spells (`Rune Prison`, `Solari Shieldbearer`) to shut down
   the opponent's best attacker or defender before combat.
2. Every stun triggers `Leona - Radiant Dawn`'s buff — spread buffs onto
   units that benefit most, like `Wizened Elder` or anything with Shield.
3. Play `Leona - Determined` when you can immediately attack into a
   battlefield and stun a blocker, clearing the way.
4. Hold `Eclipse Herald` as a payoff: once it's on board, every stun spell
   effectively grants it an extra attack.
5. Use `Solari Chief` to convert an existing stun into a kill, or to open a
   fresh stun if nothing is stunned yet.
6. Close games with `Peak Guardian` or a buffed, Shielded board that the
   opponent can't profitably attack into.

## Mulligan

Keep:

- one cheap stun: `Rune Prison`, `Solari Shieldbearer`, or `Facebreaker`;
- one cheap body: `Trifarian Gloryseeker` or `Solari Shieldbearer`;
- one payoff or stabilizer: `Wizened Elder`, `Leona - Determined`, or
  `Solari Chief`.

Avoid keeping `Eclipse Herald` or `Peak Guardian` without an early stun or
body to support them.

## Watch Outs

- Stun only stops combat damage "this turn" — it doesn't remove the unit or
  stop its abilities. Don't expect a stunned unit to be gone for good.
- `Facebreaker` stuns a friendly unit too. Only use it on a unit that doesn't
  need to attack or defend that turn, ideally one with Shield.
- `Solari Chief`'s kill only works on an already-stunned unit; against an
  unstunned target it just stuns, so sequence your other stun effects first
  if you want the kill that turn.
- Buffs only apply once per unit ("if it doesn't have a buff") — stacking
  multiple buff sources on the same unit past the first does nothing extra
  unless a card explicitly grants an additional bonus, like `Wizened Elder`.

## Upgrade Paths

- Add `Leona - Zealot` (Calm, 6-cost) for a top-end unit that punishes an
  opponent who is close to winning.
- Add more Hidden/Reaction stun spells if the meta rewards holding up
  interaction during the opponent's turn.
- For a more advanced version, build around `Karma - Channeler`'s
  recycle-into-buff engine, which asks for tighter resource sequencing than
  this beginner list.
