## Why This Is Advanced

The deck's power is in sequencing, not raw stats. Gold gear tokens are only
worth full value if you crack them at the right moment (during a showdown, to
cast an otherwise-unaffordable spell, or right before end of turn so they
don't sit unused). `Renata Glasc - Industrialist`'s "tokens enter ready" turns
every Gold, Recruit, and Sand Soldier token into an immediately usable
resource or body, which means every turn has a real decision about what to
crack now versus hold. The legend's own "Gold adds an extra Energy while your
score is within 3 of Victory" window means the correct line changes as the
game state changes — a pilot has to track their own score, not just the
board, to sequence correctly.

## How To Play

1. Prioritize early Gold generators (`Plundering Poro`, `Honest Broker`,
   `Trifarian Gloryseeker`) to build a token backlog before the champion
   lands.
2. Play `Renata Glasc - Industrialist` as soon as it's live so every token you
   make afterward enters ready and is immediately usable.
3. Use the legend's hold trigger every turn you hold a battlefield — it's
   free value and stacks with `Blue Sentinel` for a double Gold turn.
4. Track your own score. Once you're within 3 points of the Victory Score,
   every Gold token effectively adds 2 Energy instead of 1 — replan your
   sequencing around that spike rather than cracking Gold early out of habit.
5. Save `Trove Golem` for a turn where you can immediately spend or hold four
   ready Gold tokens; playing it into an empty board wastes the tempo.
6. Use `Zaun Punk` or `Pickpocket` to turn a token that's outlived its combat
   usefulness into a fresh Gold instead of holding a dead card.

## Mulligan

Keep:

- one early Gold generator: `Plundering Poro`, `Honest Broker`, or `Trifarian
  Gloryseeker`;
- one piece of removal: `Blood Money`, `Wages of Pain`, or `Deadly Flourish`;
- `Renata Glasc - Industrialist` only if you also have an early generator to
  make tokens worth readying.

Avoid keeping `Trove Golem` or `Eminent Benefactor` without early plays — they
need the board to already be developing.

## Watch Outs

- Gold tokens that sit unspent are wasted tempo once your opponent removes
  the units that would use their Energy. Crack them proactively, not only
  when forced.
- The legend's bonus Energy on Gold only applies within 3 points of Victory
  Score — do not expect the extra value before then.
- `Card Sharp` can hand your opponent a Gold token too. Only play it when your
  board state benefits more from the extra tokens than theirs does.
- This deck has almost no direct large-body removal beyond `Deadly Flourish`
  and `Wages of Pain`. Save those for the opponent's biggest threat.

## Upgrade Paths

- Add `Blue Sentinel` copies or another hold-doubler if the meta rewards
  sitting on a battlefield.
- Add hard removal (`Solari Chief`, `Sandshifter`) if opponents present large
  early threats this list can't answer.
- For a lower-complexity version, cut `Card Sharp` and `Zaun Punk` (both need
  a read on the board to use well) for more straightforward Gold generators.
