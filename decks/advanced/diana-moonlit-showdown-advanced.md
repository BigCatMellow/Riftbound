## Why This Is An Advanced Deck

The deck deliberately shifts all its action into showdowns, then stacks
advantages that exist only there:

- `Diana - Scorn of the Moon`: a reaction-speed 1 Energy each turn,
  showdown-only — over a game, that's 5-8 free Energy the opponent
  never sees in your bank;
- `Moonfall` (signature): pick a battlefield where you have units,
  drag up to one enemy unit there — the showdown happens where and
  when you chose, with your walls already home;
- `Diana - Lunari` (chosen): when a showdown begins at her battlefield,
  pay 1 → Predict, and draw the top card if it's a spell — a card per
  fight;
- `Syndra - Transcendent`: while she's in a showdown, your spells gain
  [Repeat] 2 Energy + Chaos Rune — the Legend's banked Energy pays the
  repeats;
- `Evelynn - Entrancing` from facedown drags a second enemy in;
  `Bone Skewer` makes them deploy a hand unit pre-stunned into your
  trap;
- the [Hidden] suite (`Blastcone Fae`, `Windsinger`, `Bone Skewer`,
  `Mushroom Pouch`'s draw) plays for 0 Energy at reaction speed — the
  showdown turns cost you almost nothing.

## Legality Notes

- Domain identity: Diana - Scorn of the Moon is Mind/Chaos. Every main
  deck card is single-domain Mind, single-domain Chaos, or the
  Mind/Chaos Signature card `Moonfall`.
- Copy-limit check: `Diana - Lunari` has 1 chosen-champion copy plus 2
  main deck copies (3 total, at the limit). `Diana - No Longer Human`
  is a different card name. No other card exceeds 3 copies.
- Signature check: `Moonfall` (UNL-198) is Diana's own signature
  (printed directly after the Legend, UNL-197); 3 copies sit exactly at
  the 3-signature limit and match the Legend's tag.
- Banned/errata check: no card used here is marked Banned in the local
  card database.
- Champion tag: `Diana - Lunari` and `Diana - No Longer Human` are
  inferred to carry the `Diana` champion tag from card name and the
  Legend's own name, per this project's convention. The source export
  has no explicit tag column — confirm the printed tag before
  tournament play.

## How To Play

1. Set the stage: hidden cards at your battlefields (`Blastcone Fae`,
   `Windsinger`, `Bone Skewer`, `Evelynn`) and `Mushroom Pouch` paying
   you for the setup.
2. Bank the Legend's Energy every turn — even with nothing planned, it
   accumulates toward the showdown where it all gets spent.
3. Start fights on your terms: `Moonfall` an enemy into your trap
   battlefield, or reveal `Evelynn` to drag one in. `Diana - Lunari`
   pays a card the moment the showdown begins.
4. Fight with reactions, not bodies: -Might tricks and stuns funded by
   banked Energy mean your 2-drops beat their 5-drops at night.
5. `Syndra` turns showdown turns exponential: with her in the fight and
   Marai Spire down, every trick repeats for 1 + Chaos Rune.
6. `Crescent Guardian` and `Diana - No Longer Human` reinforce at
   reaction speed — the board the opponent counted before attacking is
   never the board they fight.

## Mulligan

Keep:

- one hidden unit (`Blastcone Fae`, `Windsinger`, `Evelynn`);
- one cheap reaction (`Stupefy`, `Existential Dread`, `Frigid Touch`);
- `Mushroom Pouch` or `Diana - Lunari` as the engine opener.

Avoid keeping `Syndra` in the opener — she's the crescendo, not the
overture.

## Watch Outs

- The Legend's Energy is **showdown-only** — whether it persists across
  turns if unspent is not settled by the local rules extract. Play
  conservatively (assume it expires like other added resources at
  phase/turn end) and bank it only when a fight is live or imminent,
  per this project's explicit-ambiguity practice.
- `Moonfall` moves **up to one** enemy — it cannot drag two, and it
  needs your units at the chosen battlefield first.
- `Syndra`'s Repeat requires her **in the showdown** — in base or at a
  quiet battlefield she's a vanilla 6/6.
- `Diana - Lunari`'s trigger is per showdown **here** — fights
  elsewhere pay nothing; position her where Moonfall points.
- Hidden cards cost Any Rune to hide (1 Energy with no Teemo-style
  discount in this shell) — the traps are prepaid, not free.
- `Bone Skewer` lets the *opponent* choose which hand unit to deploy —
  it forces a stunned arrival, not a specific victim.

## Upgrade Paths

- `Guerilla Warfare` is Teemo's signature and illegal here; hidden-card
  recursion has to come from redraws, not recycling.
- `Katarina - Reckless` is Fury and off-domain; the ready-on-hide
  payoff can't port into this pair.
- `Smoke and Mirrors` (swap two of your units' positions, draw) adds
  trap-repositioning for a version that leans harder on facedown bluffs.
- `Thousand-Tailed Watcher` (-3 to all enemies on play) is the
  top-end shrink if showdowns keep going long.
