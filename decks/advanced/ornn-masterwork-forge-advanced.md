## Why This Is An Advanced Deck

Gear decks die to two things: running out of hands to hold the swords,
and paying full price twice. This build answers both structurally:

- `Ornn - Fire Below the Mountain` adds Any Rune at reaction speed,
  usable only for gear — a free gear-only discount every turn that
  never competes with your spell mana;
- `Gearhead` doubles every attached Equipment's Might bonus:
  `Rabadon's Deathcrown` (+3) becomes +6, `World Atlas` (+2) becomes
  +4 — one 5-cost body wearing two masterworks swings for 3+6+6;
- `Ornn - Forge God` counts *all* friendly gear (attached or not): with
  a normal board he's an 8-10 Might [Deflect 2] Weaponmaster;
- `Jayce - Man of Progress` cheats the expensive masterworks in: kill a
  spent `Poro Snax`, play a 4-cost signature from hand for free;
- `Pit Crew` readies every time you play gear, `Dropboarder` enters
  ready with two gear out, and `Shurelya's Requiem` readies the whole
  team on arrival — the armory generates its own attack steps;
- `Aspiring Engineer` and `Ornn - Blacksmith` rebuy and dig so the
  forge never cools.

## Legality Notes

- Domain identity: Ornn - Fire Below the Mountain is Calm/Mind. Every
  main deck card is single-domain Calm, single-domain Mind, or one of
  the three Calm/Mind Signature masterworks.
- Copy-limit check: `Ornn - Forge God` has 1 chosen-champion copy plus 2
  main deck copies (3 total, at the limit). Each masterwork is [Unique]
  (1 copy maximum by its own text). No other card exceeds 3 copies.
- Signature check: `Forgefire Cape` (SFD-190), `Rabadon's Deathcrown`
  (SFD-191), and `Shurelya's Requiem` (SFD-192) are all Ornn's own
  signatures (printed directly after the Legend, SFD-189); 1+1+1 = 3
  total sits exactly at the 3-signature limit and all match the Legend's
  tag.
- Banned/errata check: no card used here is marked Banned in the local
  card database. Note: `Forgefire Cape` and `Rabadon's Deathcrown` carry
  no rules text beyond [Unique] and their equip lines in the local
  export — they are treated here as pure stat sticks (Might 3 each). If
  the physical cards have additional text, update this file.
- Champion tag: `Ornn - Forge God` and `Ornn - Blacksmith` are inferred
  to carry the `Ornn` champion tag from card name and the Legend's own
  name, per this project's convention. The source export has no explicit
  tag column — confirm the printed tag before tournament play.

## How To Play

1. Turn 1-2: cheap gear (`Poro Snax`, `Cloth Armor`, `Doran's Shield`)
   plus `Apprentice Smith`. Spend the Legend's rune every turn — it's
   free money that only buys gear.
2. Build toward the multiplier turn: `Gearhead` (Accelerated if
   possible), then move masterworks onto him. Two masterworks make a
   3-Might body swing for 15.
3. `Ornn - Forge God` is the alternative tall plan when Gearhead draws
   removal — he scales off gear *count*, so even six 1-cost gears make
   him a 10-Might Deflect 2 wall.
4. `Jayce` wants a spent `Poro Snax` in play and a masterwork in hand —
   use Snax's sacrifice draw *before* he arrives so the kill costs
   nothing.
5. `Shurelya's Requiem` is the attack-step generator: play it after
   your units traded, ready everyone, swing again.
6. `Veiled Temple` conquest resets `Heart of Dark Ice` and `Energy
   Conduit` — with `Prize of Progress` out, each reset is also +1s.

## Mulligan

Keep:

- two 1-cost gear;
- one gear-payoff body (`Pit Crew`, `Apprentice Smith`, `Aspiring
  Engineer`);
- `Premonition` or `Meditation`.

Avoid keeping both `Ornn - Forge God` and `Gearhead` in the same opener
— the deck needs the forge running before the payoffs land.

## Watch Outs

- The Legend's rune is **gear-only** — it cannot pay for units or
  spells, so don't count it in spell math.
- `Gearhead` doubles the **base** Might bonus of Equipment attached to
  *him* only; moving the sword elsewhere halves it back.
- The masterworks are [Unique]: one copy each, and `Aspiring Engineer` /
  `Ornn - Blacksmith` are the only ways to see a killed one again.
- `Forgefire Cape` and `Rabadon's Deathcrown` have no text beyond stats
  in the local card export (see Legality Notes) — assume stat sticks
  until the printed cards say otherwise.
- `Jayce`'s free gear play is from **hand** — it cannot cheat a
  masterwork out of the trash.
- Gear-hate cards (`Detonate`, `Thermo Beam`, `Acceptable Losses`) hit
  this deck harder than any other in the lab; spread attachments across
  bearers when the opponent holds Fury mana.

## Upgrade Paths

- `Sterak's Gage` (+3 Quick-Draw, Calm) is the fourth big sword if the
  signature slots feel too slow.
- `The Zero Drive` adds a banish-and-mass-replay engine for grindy
  matchups.
- `Temporal Portal` (give the next spell Repeat = its cost) turns
  `Premonition` into "draw 6" in a spell-lighter build.
- `Sona - Harmonious` (ready 4 runes at end of turn if at a battlefield)
  effectively doubles the Legend's economy in a Calm-heavier version.
