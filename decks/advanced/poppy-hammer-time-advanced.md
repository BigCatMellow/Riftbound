## Why This Is An Advanced Deck

Unlike Master Yi (level thresholds) and Kha'Zix (XP burst), this deck
treats XP as a flexible spending currency with competing bills:

- `Poppy - Keeper of the Hammer`: hold → 1 XP, every battlefield, every
  turn; spend 3 XP → draw. The floor is "holding two battlefields pays
  two-thirds of a card per turn";
- `Poppy - Paragon` (chosen): enters ready plus 3 free XP whenever an
  opponent is within 3 of winning — the comeback clause;
- `Poppy - Defender of the Meek`: 3 XP makes her a 3-Energy 6/5
  Ambush Tank — the wallet becomes tempo;
- `Safety Inspector`: everyone kills a unit — unless you paid 3 XP, in
  which case only *they* do;
- `Blood Rose` (gear): pay 1 on each unit play → 1 XP, and 3 XP →
  ready a unit — a second faucet and a second bill;
- `Enthralling Protector`, `Crowd Favorite`, `Voracious Gromp`,
  `Demacian Diplomat`, `Scrutinizing Sergeant`: [Hunt] and on-play XP
  keep deposits coming from combat too;
- level checks (`Bandle Soldier` ready at 3+, `Gemhand Hunter` +1 at
  6+, `Concentrate` cheaper at 6+) reward *not* spending — the deck's
  hard decisions are real.

## Legality Notes

- Domain identity: Poppy - Keeper of the Hammer is Body/Order. Every
  main deck card is single-domain Body, single-domain Order, or the
  Body/Order Signature card `Keeper's Verdict`.
- Copy-limit check: `Poppy - Paragon` has 1 chosen-champion copy plus 2
  main deck copies (3 total, at the limit). `Poppy - Defender of the
  Meek` is a different card name. No other card exceeds 3 copies.
- Signature check: `Keeper's Verdict` (UNL-204) is Poppy's own
  signature (printed directly after the Legend, UNL-203); 3 copies sit
  exactly at the 3-signature limit and match the Legend's tag.
- Banned/errata check: no card used here is marked Banned in the local
  card database.
- Champion tag: `Poppy - Paragon` and `Poppy - Defender of the Meek`
  are inferred to carry the `Poppy` champion tag from card name and the
  Legend's own name, per this project's convention. The source export
  has no explicit tag column — confirm the printed tag before
  tournament play.

## How To Play

1. Establish two holds early (`Towering Combatant`, `Gemhand Hunter`,
   `Enthralling Protector`) — the Legend's income scales with
   battlefields held, and everything else follows from income.
2. Budget XP each turn *before* spending: is this turn's 3 XP a card
   (Legend), a discount (`Defender of the Meek`), a one-sided sweep
   (`Safety Inspector`), or a ready (`Blood Rose`)? The bills compete —
   that's the deck.
3. `Keeper's Verdict` to the **top** of their deck against tempo
   (they redraw the same card); to the **bottom** against value
   engines (it's gone).
4. Respect the level checks: at 5 XP with `Bandle Soldier` in hand,
   holding one more turn (6 XP) is often better than any purchase.
5. `Scrutinizing Sergeant` after a wide turn can deposit 4-5 XP at
   once — the turn before a `Defender of the Meek` + `Safety
   Inspector` double play.
6. `Poppy - Paragon` is your catch-up: she's at her best exactly when
   you're losing, so don't burn her early in games you're winning.

## Mulligan

Keep:

- one wall (`Towering Combatant`, `Gemhand Hunter`);
- one XP source (`Demacian Diplomat`, `Blood Rose`,
  `Shepherd's Heirloom`);
- `Soul Harvest` or `Keeper's Verdict` against aggression.

Avoid keeping `Scrutinizing Sergeant` or double 5-drops — income
before purchases.

## Watch Outs

- XP is a **single shared pool** — every spender drains the same
  account, and the level checks (`Bandle Soldier`, `Gemhand Hunter`,
  `Concentrate`) read the *current* balance: spending down from 6 XP
  turns them off mid-turn.
- The Legend's draw exhausts her — one purchase per turn from that
  bill, no matter how rich you are.
- [Hunt] pays on conquer **or hold** — but holding requires surviving
  the turn there; the walls aren't decoration.
- `Safety Inspector` unpaid is symmetric — never play her without
  either 3 XP or a board where the trade favors you.
- `Keeper's Verdict` doesn't kill: a deck with recursion or Vision
  effects shrugs off bottom-of-deck slower than you'd hope; the card
  buys *time*, not card advantage.
- `Poppy - Paragon`'s trigger checks the opponent's score **on play**
  — she can't be pre-deployed and still collect.

## Upgrade Paths

- `Decisive Strike`, `Riposte`, and `Showstopper` are Garen's,
  Fiora's, and Sett's signatures — Body/Order is the most signature-
  crowded pair in the lab, and none of them can port here.
- `Herald of Spring` is Calm and illegal; more XP faucets must come
  from Body/Order Hunt units.
- `Wily Newtfish` (+1 and Ganking on XP-gain turns) crosses over from
  the Miss Fortune shell for a more aggressive build.
- `Targonian Visionary` (+4 at Level 11) is the top-end if games run
  long enough to matter.
