## Why This Is An Advanced Deck

The deck is built so its own top-deck is a resource, and every layer
rewards knowing what's up there:

- `Rek'Sai - Void Burrower` (Legend): conquer → exhaust → reveal top 2,
  banish one and play it (free), recycle the other;
- `Rek'Sai - Swarm Queen`: her attack does the same before combat even
  resolves — a free blocker-eater arriving mid-swing;
- `Void Rush` (signature): the cascade as a 2-cost spell, with a
  2-Energy discount on what it flips;
- `Rek'Sai - Breacher` (chosen): friendly units played from anywhere
  other than hand have [Accelerate] — the cascaded unit can pay
  1 + Fury Rune to enter **ready** and keep fighting;
- `Undertitan`: "As I'm revealed from your deck, Add 2 Energy" — the
  cascade literally pays you for finding him, and his on-play gives the
  team +2 this turn;
- `The Candlelit Sanctum` (battlefield): every conquer filters the top
  two cards — you stack your own cascade;
- `Magma Wurm` and the enters-ready bodies (`Eager Drakehound`,
  `Vanguard Attendant`) keep the conquer count high so the engine
  triggers every single turn.

## Legality Notes

- Domain identity: Rek'Sai - Void Burrower is Fury/Order. Every main
  deck card is single-domain Fury, single-domain Order, or the
  Fury/Order Signature card `Void Rush`.
- Copy-limit check: `Rek'Sai - Breacher` has 1 chosen-champion copy
  plus 2 main deck copies (3 total, at the limit). `Rek'Sai - Swarm
  Queen` is a different card name. No other card exceeds 3 copies.
- Signature check: `Void Rush` (SFD-188) is Rek'Sai's own signature
  (printed directly after the Legend, SFD-187); 3 copies sit exactly at
  the 3-signature limit and match the Legend's tag. (The lab's Vi deck
  once ran this card illegally — see that file's changelog note.)
- Banned/errata check: no card used here is marked Banned in the local
  card database.
- Champion tag: `Rek'Sai - Breacher` and `Rek'Sai - Swarm Queen` are
  inferred to carry the `Rek'Sai` champion tag from card name and the
  Legend's own name, per this project's convention. The source export
  has no explicit tag column — confirm the printed tag before
  tournament play.

## How To Play

1. Attack early and often — every conquer with the Legend ready is a
   free card on the board. The deck's removal (`Hextech Ray`, pumps)
   exists to make conquers happen, not to control the game.
2. Sequence at `The Candlelit Sanctum` when possible: conquer there
   first to stack the top two, then cascade *knowing* what's coming.
3. `Rek'Sai - Breacher` early changes everything the cascade flips:
   pay the Accelerate tax and the free unit fights the same turn.
4. `Rek'Sai - Swarm Queen`'s cascade resolves on attack — before
   blocks matter — so her reveals can reinforce the exact battlefield
   she's attacking.
5. Save `Void Rush` for turns without a conquer, or to dig for
   `Magma Wurm` / `Undertitan` before a big swing.
6. `Right of Conquest` after a two-conquer turn is draw 3+ — the deck's
   only pure refuel, timed after the map is yours.

## Mulligan

Keep:

- `Rek'Sai - Breacher` (the deck's best turn-1/2 play);
- one cheap attacker or enters-ready body (`Eager Drakehound`,
  `Dangerous Duo`);
- one pump (`Cleave`, `Blood Rush`) to force the first conquer.

Avoid keeping `Magma Wurm` or double 6-drops — the cascade is supposed
to find the top end, not your opening hand.

## Watch Outs

- **The Legend exhausts per cascade** — one per turn, so spread
  conquers across turns rather than stacking three into one.
- **Red Brambleback + the Legend is flagged, not resolved**: his
  "conquer effects trigger an additional time" meets an effect whose
  cost is exhausting the Legend. Whether the extra trigger works
  without a second exhaust is not settled by the local rules extract —
  play it as *no second cascade* (the cost can't be paid twice) until
  confirmed, per this project's explicit-ambiguity practice. His buff
  clause and other conquer effects (Kai'Sa's draw, Qiyana's choice)
  double cleanly.
- The cascade **plays** what it flips: spells revealed can't be played
  this way in any obvious mode — prefer banishing units; if both
  reveals are spells, the recycle still filters. (The Legend's text
  says "play it" without restriction; treat spell flips conservatively
  and note outcomes if simulated.)
- `Perched Grimwyrm` is dead in hand until you've conquered *this
  turn* — it's a follow-up, never an opener.
- `Undertitan`'s 2 Energy triggers on being **revealed from the deck**
  (cascade, Void Rush, Candlelit Sanctum) — not on being drawn
  normally.
- Cascaded units enter during your attack step at the Legend's timing —
  they arrive at the *conquered* battlefield's fight only via Swarm
  Queen; the Legend's unit arrives after the conquer resolves.

## Upgrade Paths

- `Rell - Magnetic` and an Equipment splash reward the cascade finding
  gear-ready bodies.
- `Brynhir Thundersong` (opponents can't play cards this turn) protects
  the alpha-strike turn in reactive metas.
- `Noxus Hopeful` and `Battering Ram` get cheaper on cascade turns if
  the deck adds card-count payoffs.
- If the Red Brambleback ruling resolves in favor of double cascades,
  move him to 3 copies and treat his battlefield as the primary target
  every turn.
